Planning

Stage 1

Planning is the first stage of the software development life cycle and arguably the most important as it sets the foundation for the entire development process. It defines the scope of the project, the objectives, the feasibility as well as the resources which will be involved.

Inputs - Client specifications, stakeholder requirements, initial project consultations, budgets

Outputs - Project roadmap, timelines, risk assessment, feasibility report

People Involved - Stakeholders, clients, project managers, business analysts, senior designers and developers
