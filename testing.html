Testing

Stage 5

Before being deployed, the software is tested to ensure all functionality works as set out in the design stage, as well as the accessibility of the software. The speed, responsiveness and security will also be tested prior to deployment. In the case of a website, testing will also be done on readability and seo readiness.

Inputs - Completed software, staging environment, client requirements

Outputs - Bug reports, test results, accessibility report, seo report, readability report, performance audits

People Involved - Developers, project manager, client
