Requirements

Stage 2

The second stage of the software development life cycle is the requirements stage.This is the stage where the needs of the users, clients and stakeholders are assessed in order to determine what the software intends to achieve. The goal of this stage is to achieve a set of agreed-upon and clear set of both functional and non-functional requirements which will support the project moving forward.

Inputs - Meetings, consultations, goals, industry standards

Outputs - Sitemaps, specification documents, feature lists

People Involved - Project managers, developers, designers, client
