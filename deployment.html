Deployment

Stage 6

After the testing is complete and any necessary changes have been made, the approved software will be deployed into a live environment. Depending on the nature of the software, this process may involve hosting and domains, for example.

Inputs - Final approved software, deployment checklist

Outputs - Live software, backup, deployment logs

People Involved - Developers, project manager, client
