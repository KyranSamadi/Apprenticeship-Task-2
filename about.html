About Me

Learn more about me, my goals and my current job role as I embark on my apprenticeship

Overview

I have been working as a software developer at Expect Best for around a month. My love for software development came from my uncle who has always worked in IT, and also the challenging and rewarding nature of the field.

Goals

My main goal I am looking to achieve from this apprenticeship is to gain the necessary skills to obtain a full-time software development job role. This will involve learning new programming languages and skills such as application development.

Job Role

The job role I am currently working in is Apprentice Web Developer at Expect Best based in Poole, Dorset. We are an agency who provide services such as web development, web hosting, digital marketing and application development. This will allow me to learn a number of valuable skills.
